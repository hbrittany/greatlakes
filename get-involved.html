Adobt-a-Beach

Adopt-a-Beach™ is the Alliance's premier volunteer program, with nearly 13,000 participants ranging from individuals and families to schools and businesses. More than just a beach sweep, Adopt-a-Beach™ teams clear litter, monitor shorelines and complete a beach health assessment that includes science-based observations and testing. Teams collect information and enter it into our Adopt-a-Beach™ online system to share with local beach authorities, educate the public and improve beaches.

Great Lakes Education

The Alliance for the Great Lakes is dedicated to fostering an ethic of learning, appreciation and care for the Great Lakes. We offer two educational programs: Great Lakes in My World, an educator resource for kindergarten through 12th-grade that addresses Next Generation Science, Common Core and Great Lakes state learning standards, and Adopt-a-Beach™, a service learning and citizen science program tailored to all ages.

Community Organizations

From community-based environmental organizations to religious groups, trade associations, museums, schools and sporting clubs, more than 200 organizations have worked with the Alliance on behalf of the Great Lakes.
Support policies that benefit the Great Lakes. Increase your organization's impact by joining voices with citizens and groups across the region. Fulfill your service mission by raising funds for the Alliance. A donation of $100 or more entitles your organization to a one-year membership.

Our Business Partners

Participate in a cause that's important to you, your business and the 40 million people who rely on the Great Lakes. Attract new customers and benefit the Great Lakes by sponsoring an Alliance special event. Or host an event at your business and donate a percentage of your sales to the Alliance.
